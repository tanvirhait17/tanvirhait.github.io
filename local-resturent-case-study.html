Local SEO Success: Spice & Spoon Restaurant

Client Overview

  • Business Name: Spice & Spoon
  • Industry: Restaurant (Bangladeshi & Indian Cuisine)
  • Location: Dhanmondi, Dhaka, Bangladesh
  • Service Focus: Dine-in, Takeaway, and Online Orders

Objective

  • Increase foot traffic
  • Improve visibility in Google Maps and “near me” searches
  • Generate more calls and orders through local search
  • They had no prior SEO, limited online presence, and very few customer reviews.

Challenges

  • Unclaimed and unoptimized Google Business Profile (GBP)
  • Poor presence in Google Maps and local 3-pack
  • Inconsistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across the web
  • No local backlinks or citations
  • No structured data (Schema) or localized on-page content

Strategy & Implementation

  1. Google Business Profile Optimization
    • Claimed and verified the listing
    • Added accurate hours, business info, menu link, and high-quality images
    • Chose correct categories: Bangladeshi restaurant, Takeout restaurant
    • Posted weekly updates and offers to boost engagement
  2. On-Page Local SEO
    • Optimized homepage and key pages with keywords like: "Best restaurant in Dhanmondi", "Indian food near me"
    • Created a dedicated “Location” and “Contact Us” page
    • Added LocalBusiness Schema using JSON-LD
  3. NAP Consistency & Citations
    • Fixed inconsistent listings across platforms (Facebook, Yelp, TripAdvisor, BD Yellow Pages)
    • Created new citations in 30+ local directories
    • Used BrightLocal to track and manage citations
  4. Review Generation System
    • Set up automated review request QR code on dine-in receipts
    • Incentivized reviews with small offers (e.g., 10% off next visit)
    • Replied to all reviews with thoughtful and personalized responses
  5. Local Backlinks & PR
    • Reached out to local food bloggers for backlinks
    • Submitted to Dhaka Food Guide and Top 10 Restaurants listicles
    • Sponsored a local food event and earned a mention on an event site

Results (After 3 Months)

Website Clicks from GBP 50/month 900+/month +1,700%

Key Takeaways

  • Google Business Profile is a critical asset for restaurants and must be optimized fully.
  • Consistent local citations and schema markup signal trust to Google.
  • Positive reviews and backlinks from local sites drastically improve authority and rankings.
  • Localized content on the website helped capture organic search and voice search traffic.

Conclusion

With the right local SEO strategy, even a small local restaurant can dominate search results and significantly grow traffic, calls, and orders. In just 90 days, this restaurant went from nearly invisible to a local search leader in their area.
